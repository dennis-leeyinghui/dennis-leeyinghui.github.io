Becoming New Parents

My wife and I are expecting our newborn in two months. Nothing is more exciting than the fact that you are going to meet the cutest person in the world very soon.

However, it is undeniable that heavy financial stress could strike much earlier that the little baby arrives.

I’ve gathered some tips from myself and online communities on some frugal tips to welcome your babies:

  1. Calm down. Don’t do impulsive shopping.

  2. Buy second-handed clothes, as infants outgrow their clothes very quickly.

  3. Talk to friends & neighbours. See if they have any baby products for you.

  4. Play white noise music to infants and they can fall asleep very quickly.

  5. Baby stroller may not be necessary, if you drive everywhere instead of walking.

  6. Go for breastfeeding and self-made baby food, which is healthier for both moms and babies.
