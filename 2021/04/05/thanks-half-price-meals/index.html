Thank you, 50% off your meals!

In order to support local restaurants, the PEI government recently rolled out a temporary PEI Dine In & Save promotion program, subsidizing diners 50% of their bills at designated restaurants with a ceiling of CA$15/person.

Now the program is over. Here are some personal recaps of the program:

  • Went to 2 Irish restaurant + 1 Japanese restaurant + 1 Iranian restaurant
  • Saved roughly CA$60 / person
  • Had some of the best burgers & ramen in town

Pros of the program:

  • Restored indoor dining confidence COVID-19.
  • Provided restaurant owners with some off-season businesses.
  • Improved islanders’ sense of belongings.

Cons of the program:

  • Many restaurants forgot to take 50% off their bills.
  • Some dishes were hidden from the menus and caused diners’ disappointment.
  • Not all restaurants in PEI were covered. You needed to check constantly for the updated list of participants.

Recommendation:

  • Extend the program duration to benefit more islanders.
  • Provide example scenarios to customers to align with their expectations. (e.g. Is a baby eligible for the 50% discount too?)
  • Allow extra discounts for cultural food restaurants to promote cultural diversity.
